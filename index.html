What is a Dummy Flight Ticket?

A dummy flight ticket is nothing but a flight reservation that you can make without paying the full price of the travel ticket. It is, basically, a document that has the flight itinerary details but is not a confirmed ticket.

Your dummy ticket (or flight reservation) will include:

  • Departure Date
  • Arrival Date
  • Passenger Full Name
  • The Name of the Airlne
  • Flight Details with Airline Number
  • Booking reference/Confirmation Number/PNR.

Why do I need a Dummy Ticket?

Applying for visas is the primary purpose of a dummy ticket. Dummy ticket is also used to clear immigration while going on a visit visa to another country as a time proof of return. Here are some uses for a dummy ticket:

  • Visa submission/Application
  • Immigration Clearencce or Proof of return or Onward travel
  • Expedite US and other countries passport renewal
  • Show company HR/ Manager for leave and other purposes
  • Exit visa procedures in many GCC countries
  • Rent a car
