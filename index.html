What is a Dummy Flight Ticket?

A dummy flight ticket is nothing but a flight reservation that you can make without paying the full price of the travel ticket. It is, basically, a document that has the flight itinerary details but is not a confirmed ticket.

Your dummy ticket (or flight reservation) will include:

  • Departure Date
  • Arrival Date
  • Passenger Full Name
  • The Name of the Airlne
  • Flight Details with Airline Number
  • Booking reference/Confirmation Number/PNR.
